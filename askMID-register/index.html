askMID Registration – Do You Need to Sign Up?

If you are looking to register or create an account on askMID, it's important to know that normal users do not need to register to check vehicle insurance details.

Who Needs to Register on askMID?

The askMID system is mainly used by:

  • Insurance companies
  • Fleet or business vehicle operators
  • Data partners or system developers

These users may need access to update records, manage large fleets, or integrate with the MID system. For this, registration and login are required through the Motor Insurers’ Bureau (MIB) system.

Do You Need to Register to Check Your Insurance?

No. If you want to check your own vehicle insurance status, there is no need to register. Just follow these steps:

  1. Go to https://www.midis.org.uk/register
  2. Enter your vehicle registration number
  3. Accept the terms and click "Check This Vehicle"

This service is free and does not require any signup or account.

How to Register for Business or Fleet Use

If you are a fleet operator or insurer and need to access the system, you must contact the Motor Insurers’ Bureau (MIB) directly to request access.

Visit the official MIB site here: https://www.mib.org.uk

Need Help?

If you're unsure whether you need to register, visit our Help page or see the Check Insurance guide for more details.

Summary

  • Regular users do not need to register to use askMID
  • Vehicle insurance checks are free and public
  • Registration is only for businesses, insurers, or fleet managers
  • Contact MIB if you require access to manage records
