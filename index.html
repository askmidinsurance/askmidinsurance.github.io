Frequently Asked Questions

What is askMID Insurance?

askMID is a website where you can check if a vehicle is insured in the UK. It is managed by the Motor Insurers' Bureau (MIB) and shows information from the Motor Insurance Database (MID), which is used by the police and DVLA to make sure vehicles have insurance.

When you buy insurance, it can take between 2 to 7 days for it to be reflected on the MID. Be aware that some short-term policies might not show up at all if they expire before the system updates. Always keep your insurance documents as proof, just in case!
